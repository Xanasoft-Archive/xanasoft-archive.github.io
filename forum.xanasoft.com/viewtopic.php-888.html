Hello,

there is no team behind Sandboxie-Plus. David is the lead developer and there are some contributions made by others. So only David is the one who made a decision about it. He has stated that to be not the case here and here. On GitHub, he stated not having access to the list of previous users and thereby being unable to verify it, even if he was going to be willing. He's not the legal successor.

Ronen Tzur sold to Invincea Inc. who sold to the Sophos Group, who before discontinuing the project released the source code. This code was forked and Sandboxie-Plus was created by David. This is not the continuation of the original project.

Everything users had to use the old license for could and can be used free-of-charge after the open-source release, as long as that was within the code. This includes the use of being able to force programs into sandboxes and have practically unlimited sandboxes.

Sandboxie-Plus and Classic work fine without the need to spend money. After some period of time, it shows a support reminder, which appears once a month, before that gets down to once every five days. This won't get shown with a valid certificate. I had the idea to have a certificate that only suppresses the reminder, which has not been done, yet, but might be considered as the idea was not rejected, yet.

All features that require a certificate were newly introduced by David.

There are various ways to obtain a certificate.
