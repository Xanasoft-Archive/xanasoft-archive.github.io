Re: SBIE2101 - can't launch ANYTHING in ANY sandbox

  • Quote

Post by DavidXanatos » Mon Sep 06, 2021 12:47 pm

windows 11 the builds past 22000.x need an additional open IPC path

OpenIpcPath=\Sessions\*\BaseNamedObjects\SM*:WilStaging_*

with it it works fine,

this setting will be included by default in upcoming builds but till then you can add it manually to your sandboxie ini,

Cheers
David X.
