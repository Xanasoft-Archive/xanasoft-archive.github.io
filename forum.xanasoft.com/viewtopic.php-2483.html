Nope this is not a typo, lifetime is a very long time period hence its very expensive.
A pay once and some one will work for you for ever sort of arrangement is not economically viable in the long run unless is appropriately priced from the get go.

Think of all the one click hosters 200€/lifetime offers that went offline after a year or two... if someone offers you lifetime for cheep its most certainly a exit scam, or a initial marketing action for a very limited time.

The medium and large certificates allow you to use the last version that falls into their validity period for ever anyways, you need to renew your certs only to get updates, which in case of sandboxie is important though.
