i sometimes have ed2k firewalled due to neokad (moment of crazy usage of resources) core freezes and mulekad get firewalled, i usually fix this by killing both neokad and mulekad processes in task manager. otherwise close NL, check taskmanager for all NL related process to fade and restart, because you can have 2 running mulekad modules and the one from the running nl session will be firewalled.

log: settings window>interface>(GUI) "Show Log Tabs"
