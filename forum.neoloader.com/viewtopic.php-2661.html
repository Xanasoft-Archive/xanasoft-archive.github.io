Postby abel » Sun Aug 18, 2013 10:03 pm

the question is not "what to choose in the wizard," but "if choose in the wizard."
In 1st wizard these choices do not make sense, because the user will not know the difference between the choices. Provide a choice "masked" by simple voice "Low / Medium / High" with default in the midlle is an acceptable trade-off, because the user can get to make the right choice without knowing anything about what happens under the hood (SUI, Tracker, userhash, etc.), or he can leave the default choice.
If the most experienced user does not want the SUI and does not want the tracker, he can disable in the settings. But the Wizards is for newbies.
