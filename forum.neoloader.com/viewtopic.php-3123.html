Neo26 wrote:Write some examples of when this feature deleting multi file is useful.

When you are a releaser and you want to release a just on air CC licensed season of a TV series (like PIONEER ONE an original dramatic series for VODO. for example) you have a directory with episodes 1-5 and make a multi-file out of it and share as torrent.
Than episode 6 gets released, so you add the new episode to your PioneerOne directory rescan the share.
Then use the remove multi-file option to kill the multi-file without killing the sub files and you create a new multi file now of all 1-6 episodes, and each week you repeat the process until the season is complete.
