Description

The Sandboxie Plus Business License, is specifically designed to empower your business operations in a commercial setting. With this license, you are granted access for one seat for a duration of 12 months.

Should your business require licenses for multiple seats, you’ll need to purchase a separate unit for each seat. Each of these seats will be provided with a unique serial number. By entering this serial number in the user interface, you’ll obtain a unique certificate that’s exclusively tied to your current motherboard’s GUID. It’s important to note that to use this certificate, you must be operating on Sbie+ version 1.10 or a newer version.

As with all good things, this license does have an expiration – to enjoy uninterrupted access to the software, it’s essential to renew your certificate at the end of each year.
